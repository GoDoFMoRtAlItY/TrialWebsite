What We Offer

Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine cleanings to full smile makeovers, we offer everything your family needs under one roof.

🪥

General Dentistry

Routine exams, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care to keep your teeth healthy for life. We recommend check-ups every six months.

✨

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, bonding, and smile makeovers designed to give you the confidence to show off your smile.

🦷

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions that look, feel, and function just like natural teeth, restoring your smile and oral health.

😁

Orthodontics

Clear aligners and traditional braces for teens and adults. Straighten your teeth comfortably and discreetly at any age.

🧒

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, fun dental care for children of all ages. We make first visits a positive experience to set a lifelong foundation for oral health.

🚨

Emergency Dental Care

Tooth pain can't wait. We offer same-day emergency appointments for urgent dental issues including cracked teeth and severe pain.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Prasenjit Saha, DDS

I've been serving this community for over 15 years and believe that great dental care begins with listening. Every patient is unique, and your treatment plan should reflect your individual goals and concerns.
